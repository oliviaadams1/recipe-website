Browned Butter Chcoclate Chip Cookies

Image from Salt and Baker

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of baking soda
2 sticks (1 cup) of unsalted butter
200 grams of packed dark brown sugar
20 0grams of granulated sugar
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
1 tablespoon of Kosher salt
375 grams of flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Brown butter over medium heat
  3. In a small mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, white sugar, and browned butter and cream together
  4. Add in eggs and vanilla and mix until combined
  5. Add flour and baking soda until combines
  6. Add chocolate chips to the batter and mix until uniform
  7. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  8. Use a #30 scoop to measure cookie dough(about 2 tablespoons)
  9. Arrange dough onto parchment lined pan, leaving spaced for the cookies to spread out
  10. Bake cookies for 9-12 minutes or until edges begin turning golden brown
  11. Remove the cookies from oven and sprinkle with Kosher salt
  12. Let cool and enjoy!
  13. Store cookies in a sealed container for up to 3 days
