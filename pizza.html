High Protein Pizza

Image from The Big Man's World

Ingredients

Crust

70 grams of self-rising flour
12 grams of coconut flour
5 grams of nutritional yeast
Garlic Powder
Dried basil
150 grams of non-fat greek yogurt
75 grams of egg whites

Toppings

120 grams of tomato sauce seasoned to taste
56 grams of fat-free mozzarella
25 turkey pepperonis

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flours, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, and dried basil
  3. Add greek yogurt and egg whites and mix until thourougly combined
  4. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper
  5. Spread dough in a thin layer on the parchment paper
  6. Par bake the crust for 10 minutes
  7. Remove the crust from the oven and add tomato sauce, cheese, and pepperoni
  8. Place the pizza back into the over for another 10 minutes or until the cheese is meltd and the crust is golden brown
  9. Slice and enjoy!
