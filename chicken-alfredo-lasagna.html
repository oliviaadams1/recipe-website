Chicken Alfredo Lasagna

Image from Little Sunny Kitchen

Ingredients

600 grams of diced chicken breast
Italian seasoning, black pepper, red pepper flakes, and granulated garlic
400 grams of low fat cottage cheese
100 mL of milk of choice
25 grams of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
5 grams of flour mixed with water
6 lasagna sheets
180 grams of fat free mozzarella

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Season chicken to taste
  3. Cook chicken over medium to high heat until cooked through
  4. Cook lasagna sheets per package directions
  5. In a blender, combine cottage cheese, seasoning, milk, and Parmesan cheese and blend until smooth
  6. Heat the sauce over low to medium heat
  7. Add flour and water mixture to the sauce and combine until the sauce thickens
  8. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish followed by three lasagna sheets, more sauce, hald of the chicken, and half of the mozzarella
  9. Complete step 8 once more
  10. Cover and bake for 25 minutes
  11. Uncover and place in over for 10 minutes or until cheese gets golden brown
  12. Let cool and enjoy!
