Cinnamon Rolls

Image from Butternut Bakery

Ingredients

Cinnamon Roll Dough

625 grams (5 cups) of all-purpose flour
75 grams (1/3 cup) of granulated sugar
4 1/2 tsp of instant yeast
1 teaspoon of salt
355 grams (1 1/2 cups) of warm water
85 grams (6 tablespoons) of unsalted butter, softened
1 large egg

Filling

55 grams (1/4 cup) of unsalted butter, softened
5 tablespoons of cinnamon
100 grams (1/2 cup) of brown sugar

Cream Cheese Icing

4 ounces of cream cheese, room temperature
55 grams (1/4 cup) unsalted butter, softened
120 grams (1 cup) of powdered sugar
2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract

Directions

Cinnamon Roll Dough

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine suagr, yeast, salt and 250 grams (2 cups) of flour
  2. Pour warm water into dry ingredients
  3. Add an egg and mix until combined
  4. Add an additional 250 grams (2 cups) of flour and mix. The dough should be sticky at this point
  5. Add in 95 grams (3/4 cup) of flour and knead by hand until a soft, tacky dough forms. Add aditional flour if needed
  6. Let dough rest uncovered for 10 minutes
  7. After the 10 minutes is up, place the dough on a lightly floured surface and roll it into a 10 x 15 in rectange using a rolling pin
  8. Spread the room temperature butter into a thin and even layer on the dough
  9. Sprinkle with brown sugar and top with cinnamon
  10. Cut dough roll into 18 even rolls with unflavored dental floss or a knife
  11. Place rolls into two buttered 9 x 13 baking dishes
  12. Place in a warm spot and cover with a towel to rise for at least one hour
  13. Preheat oven to 350°F
  14. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until the tops are a light golden brown

Cream Cheese Icing

  1. Place cream cheese and butter into a bowl and whisk together
  2. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla and whisk until smooth
  3. Spread icing onto the warm rolls and enjoy!
