Welcome! I'm Remy

Once upon a time, I was a Parisian chef — tall hat, white coat, checked pants, the works! I left the restaurant industry in 2007 to persue a more laid back lifestyle. Now, I am cooking for my friends and family and sharing all my perfected recipes with you here.

FAQs

Who inspired you to become a chef?

Auguste Gusteau is my hero. I resonate with his inspiring quote, "Anyone can cook." After hearing of his passing, I decided to persue my dreams of becoming a chef. I even had the pleasure to cook in his restaurant, Gusteau's along side his son, Alfredo Lingunini (who is, to this day, still my best friend).

Where do you get your recipes?

Occasionally, I will adapt recipes from another source, or share a Gusteau favorite, but I develop most of my own recipes and perfect them in my home kitchen.
